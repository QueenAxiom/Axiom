# Ecosystem Reference

**Axiom Enterprises** is the parent organization.

| Brand | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Empowered At Home | AI, smart home technology, robotics, and independent living |
| EverythingInternet.ca | Canadian business directory and discovery platform |
| Brainz | Research, strategy, and knowledge-development initiative |

Each brand has its own purpose, but they support one another — e.g. someone discovers Axiom through EverythingInternet.ca, gets interested in AI through Empowered At Home, and benefits from research/strategy developed through Brainz.

One ecosystem with multiple entry points, not three unrelated businesses.
