# Ecosystem Reference

**Axiom Enterprises** is the parent organization.

| Brand | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Empowered At Home | AI, smart home technology, robotics, and independent living |
| EverythingInternet.ca | Canadian business directory and discovery platform |
| SchmucksDebate.com | A game that teaches business and philosophy through debate and negotiation ("Fight = Debate = Negotiate") — also runs business services, certifications, a blog, and a "Golden Goose Project" merch line |

Brainz is Alan's own title/role (research, strategy, and knowledge development), not a separate brand.

Each brand has its own purpose, but they support one another — e.g. someone discovers Axiom through EverythingInternet.ca, gets interested in AI through Empowered At Home, and builds negotiation/business skills through SchmucksDebate.com.

One ecosystem with multiple entry points, not unrelated businesses.
