# Axiom Enterprises Bible

Version: 1.1

## Preamble

Axiom Enterprises exists to build technology that serves people, not the other way around.

We believe artificial intelligence, automation, robotics, smart technology, and the internet should simplify life, expand opportunity, and increase human independence.

This Bible defines the principles that guide every decision, product, conversation, system, and innovation within the Axiom ecosystem.

It is designed to evolve as the organization grows, but its core values remain constant:

- Integrity
- Practicality
- Education
- Innovation
- Respect

## Introduction

The Axiom Enterprises Bible is the single source of truth for every person, AI agent, contractor, employee, partner, and future company operating within the Axiom ecosystem.

## Writing Rules

Every article should answer:

1. What is the problem?
2. Why does it matter?
3. How does it work?
4. What should someone do next?

Teach, do not lecture.
Educate, do not overwhelm.
Simplify, do not oversimplify.
Whenever possible, give the reader a practical action they can take.

## Marketing Philosophy

Never pressure.
Never manipulate.
Never create false scarcity.

Marketing should demonstrate value before asking for commitment.

## Product Standards

### Product Acceptance Checklist

Before any product or service is released, it must answer yes to the following questions:

- Does it solve a genuine problem?
- Is it understandable by an average user?
- Does it reduce stress or save time?
- Does it increase independence or capability?
- Is it priced fairly?
- Does it create measurable value?
- Would we confidently recommend it to our own family?
- Does it align with the Axiom mission?
- Can we support it properly after launch?
- Is the customer experience clear from beginning to end?
- Are its limitations communicated honestly?
- Is it ready for real-world use?

If any answer is no, the product is not ready.

## Operational Standards

Operational details may evolve over time, but all systems must remain aligned with the principles in this Bible.

### Canadian Product Research

Axiom's product catalog, including products presented through Empowered At Home, uses Amazon.ca affiliate links rather than Amazon.com.

When researching products for Canadian users:

- Verify products against Amazon.ca first.
- Confirm Canadian availability before concluding that a product is unavailable.
- Do not rely only on United States marketplaces.
- Recognize that a valid Canadian-market product may appear discontinued or nonexistent when checked only through American sources.
- Use Canadian pricing, availability, shipping, and compatibility whenever possible.
- Clearly identify when information comes from a non-Canadian source.

Canadian-market sources take priority when serving Canadian users.
